Dear Mama
the heart of everything we are
🌷
There is a kind of love that needs no words to be understood —
the kind that wakes before anyone else,
that notices when something is wrong before you speak,
that fills a room simply by being in it.

That is the love you gave us.
Quiet, boundless, patient as the seasons.
You bloomed us into who we are,
the way spring coaxes tulips from cold earth —
gently, surely, without asking for anything in return.

On this day, we give you back some of that warmth.
We see you. We treasure you.
We are so endlessly grateful to call you ours.
💖
With all our love
p.s. — the tulips are for you. they always were. 🌷
